# My Photography Journey

## How It All Started

My interest in photography began during a trip to the mountains. I was captivated by the breathtaking landscapes and wanted to preserve those moments. What started as casual smartphone photography quickly evolved into a passionate hobby.

## Learning Process

Photography has a steep learning curve, but it's incredibly rewarding. I've spent countless hours learning about:

- Exposure triangle (aperture, shutter speed, ISO)
- Composition techniques
- Lighting principles
- Post-processing workflows

## Favorite Shots

Some of my favorite photographs were taken during the golden hour, that magical time around sunset when the light bathes everything in a warm glow. There's something special about the way shadows lengthen and colors intensify during this time.
